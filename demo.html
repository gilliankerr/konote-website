This is a live registration form running on a real KoNote2 instance. Try filling it out to see the experience your participants would have.

This demo shows a simplified version. KoNote2 includes over 50 configurable fields across 9 field groups — contact details, emergency contacts, demographics, accessibility, referral info, consent forms, and more. Your agency chooses which fields and groups to include, and can add custom ones in minutes. No developer needed.
